Drive to destinations....

Automobiles and other vehicles are made available for hiring by car rental companies for a price, which is often levied either hourly or daily. Due to the ease of travelling by automobile, there is a growing need for rental cars, which has resulted in a burgeoning car rental industry. Those who possess a car may utilise car rental services when their automobiles break down or while they wait for insurance clearance, while those without a car may need to borrow a car to get where they need to go instead of utilising public transportation. The need for a well-designed and user-friendly system that car-hire firms can use to manage personnel, cars, drivers, and customers arises from the fact that the car rental industry is expanding and that the majority of companies now have many locations in different cities. This project involves developing a car rental system that small, medium, and big automobile rental companies may use.
